Current project

Łojasiewicz-type inequality for a two-variable polynomial

I study the optimal exponent β in an inequality of the form |F|^{2β} ≤ C|∇F|² near the origin for a polynomial F(x,y) with quadratic and cubic terms, using careful case classification and worst-direction analysis.
