Current project

Łojasiewicz-type inequality for a two-variable polynomial. We study the optimal exponent β in an inequality of the form |F|^{2β} ≤ C|∇F|² near the origin for F(x,y)=ax²+by²+cx³+dx²y+exy²+fy³, with careful case classification and worst-direction analysis.
